Fentanyl Overdose Predictions



Future Predictions

Explore our Future Fentanyl Death Counts Visualization, a direct approach to understanding the impending impact of the Fentanyl crisis across California from June 2023 to December 2024. This visualization is designed to present raw death counts, offering users a straightforward numerical insight into the tragedy unfolding within each county. Recognizing that rates can sometimes obscure the immediacy of the problem, we've created this tool to complement our death rate visual, making the data more accessible and comprehensible.

By selecting a specific county, the visualization narrows down to showcase data relevant to that area alone. Furthermore, a monthly filter feature allows users to tailor the time frame of interest, providing a focused view on the progression of Fentanyl-related deaths over time. This feature is invaluable for those seeking to understand the scope of the crisis at a more granular level, whether for research, policy development, or community outreach purposes.

Our aim is to deliver clarity and facilitate a deeper comprehension of the data, empowering stakeholders and the community at large to take informed actions. As you navigate through this visualization, consider the lives represented by each count and the collective effort needed to address this unprecedented challenge.
