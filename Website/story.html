Sentanyl
Rapid, localized forecasting of Fentanyl overdoses for prevention and safety

Jose DelValle - Lucy Herr - Oscar Garcia - David Daniels - Hans Hernandez
In addressing California's opioid crisis, we highlight fentanyl's pivotal role in escalating overdose deaths. This synthetic opioid, up to 100 times stronger than morphine, is a major contributor to the state's public health emergency. Our data show a dramatic rise from 87 deaths in 1999 to nearly 7000 of the over 10,000 opioid deahts in 2022, underscoring the urgent challenge at hand. The hidden danger lies in fentanyl's incredible potency, relatively low cost, and its frequent mixing with other drugs, drastically amplifying overdose risks.
Over 65% of drug overdose deaths in the last 3 years were considered preventable
We've gathered data from all over the internet and combined it here to deliver dashboards with insights into this epidemic.
Using advanced artificial intelligence models and data science techniques we were able to provide future predictions.
Our mission is to harness the power of data science and innovative machine learning techniques to analyze available data and predict Fentanyl drug overdoses and deaths. Through our predictive models and insights, we aim to provide valuable information that can contribute to preventative strategies, inform policy decisions, and ultimately, save lives affected by the opioid epidemic.
