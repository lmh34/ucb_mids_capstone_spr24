Historical Fentanyl Overdoses  

Historical Data

This visual guide offers a clear comparison of the extent of the Fentanyl crisis, allowing for an equitable analysis irrespective of the county's population size. Our dataset commences in 2016, providing a solid historical foundation to understand the progression of Fentanyl-related fatalities over time.

To ensure the utmost confidence in our projections, we've incorporated a validation phase spanning from June 2022 to June 2023. This critical period allows users to assess the accuracy of our predictive models against real-world outcomes, laying a trust foundation for our future forecasts.

With a commitment to shedding light on this urgent public health issue, our predictions extend through to December 2024. By navigating this visualization, stakeholders, policymakers, and the general public can gain valuable insights into the Fentanyl crisis's trajectory. Our goal is to empower informed decision-making and targeted interventions that could potentially save lives and alleviate the impact of Fentanyl across California.
